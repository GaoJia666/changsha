Changsha, also known as Xingcheng, is a prefecture-level city, provincial capital and mega-city under the jurisdiction of Hunan Province, located in central China, downstream of Xiangjiang River, west edge of Changliu Basin and north of eastern Hunan, between 111°53′-114°15′ East longitude and 27°51′-28°41′ North latitude. It is a subtropical monsoon climate with mild climate, abundant precipitation and hot and rainy period. As of the end of 2021, the city has 6 municipal districts, 1 county, and 2 county-level cities under its jurisdiction, with a total area of 11,819 square kilometers and a total resident population of 10,239,300.
